Project

In this e-commerce project, our customers will have a search function that allows them to easily find their products and filter them by the desired features, such as price ranges, brands, categories or specifications. I will manage the data using SQL and store customer information, product information, orders and payment details, among other data. This will allow me to quickly query and update the data. Our customers will be able to easily find their products, manage their shopping carts, and complete their payment transactions quickly using this platform. By using SQL for data management, we can effectively store and process the data.
